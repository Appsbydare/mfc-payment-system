📈 What the Sheet Needs to Do:
Group Classes
Merge GoTeamUp attendance/export data with the most recent paid membership per member.

Calculate class revenue based on the membership/package type.

Apply fixed revenue percentages to:

Landlord pay

Management pay

Private Sessions

Free or zero-value memberships require manual override

Clients with multiple profiles/packages

Multi-packs must be split over total sessions attended

Different booking methods (drop-ins vs. class packs)

Matching attendance to the correct, most recent package
